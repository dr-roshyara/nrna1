# Outcome Bonus: When and How to Award Extra Points

The outcome bonus is an optional extra award for contributions with measurable, significant impact.

---

## What is the Outcome Bonus?

A **flat point bonus** (0–200 points) awarded by admins **after** the formula is calculated.

**How it works:**
- Member logs contribution and gets initial point estimate
- You review and verify it
- If the impact was exceptional, you **add extra points**
- Final total = formula points + outcome bonus

**Example:**
- Base formula: 1,200 points
- Outcome: Helped 300 people
- Outcome bonus: +150
- Final: 1,350 points

---

## When to Award Outcome Bonus

Award an outcome bonus when the contribution achieved **measurable, documented impact beyond the normal effort.**

### Green Light (Award Bonus)

✅ **Built a water system that serves 500+ families**
- Impact: Measurable (# of families)
- Duration: Lasting (still in use)
- Scale: Significant (500+ people)
- Bonus: +150

✅ **Funded scholarships that changed 25 students' lives**
- Impact: Documented (students can testify)
- Duration: Life-changing (education)
- Scale: Significant (25 people)
- Bonus: +100

✅ **Started a health clinic in underserved area**
- Impact: Community-wide
- Duration: Ongoing
- Scale: Serves 100+ monthly
- Bonus: +150–200

✅ **Trained 50 community members in skills**
- Impact: Skills transfer (they teach others)
- Duration: Knowledge lasts forever
- Scale: 50 people directly, many indirectly
- Bonus: +100

### Yellow Light (Ask for More Info)

⚠️ **Organized a one-time workshop with 30 people**
- Impact is good but not lasting
- "Did people apply the skills? Any follow-up?"
- Probably no bonus, or +25 if documented impact

⚠️ **Created educational materials**
- Could have impact, but unknown
- "How many people use them? Do you have metrics?"
- Bonus depends on adoption/usage

### Red Light (No Bonus)

❌ **"I mentored someone for 4 hours"**
- Outcome is impossible to measure
- No bonus (already covered in formula)

❌ **"I taught a workshop"**
- No mention of outcome or impact
- No bonus (formula covers the effort)

❌ **"I organized an event"**
- Event itself is the contribution
- No bonus (formula covers it)

## How Much Bonus to Award

Use this scale:

| Impact | Scope | Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| **Local impact** | Helped 10–25 people | +25–50 |
| **Community impact** | Helped 25–100 people | +50–100 |
| **Significant impact** | Helped 100–300 people | +100–150 |
| **Transformational** | Helped 300+ OR life-changing | +150–200 |

### Examples

**+25 bonus:**
- Small mentoring group (10 people)
- Workshop with modest attendance
- Short-term project with limited reach

**+50 bonus:**
- Mentored 20 people
- Taught 25 students
- Small community project

**+100 bonus:**
- Mentored 50 people
- Taught 100 students (full semester)
- Community project serving 100+ people
- Long-term initiative (1+ year)

**+150 bonus:**
- Mentored 100+ people
- Established a lasting program
- Infrastructure serving 200+ people
- Major partnership/institution-building

**+200 bonus:**
- Transformational impact (changed lives significantly)
- Served 300+ people durably
- Built something that will last years/decades
- Institutional integration or system change

## How to Award the Bonus

### Step 1: Review the Contribution Details

During your approval review, look for:
- ✓ Outcome metrics (# of people helped)
- ✓ Evidence of impact (testimonials, data, follow-up)
- ✓ Sustainability (will it last?)
- ✓ Scale (how many people?)

### Step 2: Ask If Needed

If the impact isn't clear, **ask the member** in your review:

> "This looks like great work! To give you outcome bonus points, can you tell me:
> - How many people were directly helped?
> - What's the lasting impact?
> - Do you have any metrics or testimonials?"

### Step 3: Add the Bonus

When approving, you'll see:

```
Formula Total: 1,200 points

Outcome Bonus: [___] (0–200)

Final Total: 1,200 + ____ = ____
```

**Enter the bonus amount** and approve.

### Step 4: Notification

The system sends member a message:

> "Your contribution has been approved! You earned 1,200 formula points + 150 outcome bonus = 1,350 points total. Well done on the impact!"

## Evidence You Should See

**For +50 bonus:** Photos or brief mention  
"We served 30 people at the workshop. Photos attached."

**For +100 bonus:** Documentation  
"Taught 50 students over semester. Here's the attendance sheet."

**For +150 bonus:** Official confirmation  
"Organization letter confirming I mentored 100+ youth and 40 now work in the field."

**For +200 bonus:** Major documentation  
"Institutional partnership letter. The water system serves 500 families, 95% usage rate."

## Guidelines to Avoid Bias

The outcome bonus should be **fair, objective, and consistent**:

### Fair Criteria
- ✓ Based on **measurable impact**, not personal feelings
- ✓ Same rules for everyone (no favorites)
- ✓ Documented in the ledger
- ✓ Can be audited and reviewed

### Avoid
- ❌ Bonus because "I like this person"
- ❌ Bonus because "They've been here long"
- ❌ Bonus because of personal relationship
- ❌ Different standards for different people

### Consistency
Post your guidelines somewhere:
> "We award outcome bonus when contribution helped 25+ people with documented evidence. Bonus scale: +25 (25–50 people), +50 (50–100 people), +100 (100–300 people), +150 (300+ people)."

---

## Correcting a Mistake

If you awarded too much or too little bonus:

1. **Note it in the ledger** (don't edit)
2. **Create a correction entry** (negative adjustment)
3. **Document why** in the reason field

Example:
```
Correction Entry
User: Dr. Patel
Points: -50
Action: adjusted
Reason: "Original outcome bonus was +200. Audit found impact was 250 people, not 400. Correcting to +150."
```

Member can see the correction and understand it.

---

## Questions?

- **"Can members appeal a bonus decision?"** — Yes. If they think impact deserves more bonus, they can ask you to reconsider.
- **"What if I can't measure the impact?"** — Award no bonus. Formula already covers effort.
- **"Should I give bonus for every contribution?"** — No! Only for exceptional impact beyond normal.
- **"Can I change a bonus after approving?"** — Yes, through correction entries (never edit).
