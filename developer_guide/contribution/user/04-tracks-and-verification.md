# Tracks and Verification: Which is Right for You?

This guide helps you choose the right track and understand proof types.

---

## The Three Tracks

```
   ⚡ MICRO         📌 STANDARD        🏆 MAJOR
  Quick Work    Organized Work    Significant Work
  0–3 hours     4–40 hours        40–100+ hours
  ≤ 30 points   31–200 points     201+ points
  No proof      Light proof       Heavy proof
  ✓ Honor sys   ✓ Verified        ✓ Institutional
```

---

## Micro Track: The Honor System

### What Is It?
Small, informal contributions you do for your community. **We trust you** — no proof required.

### Best For
- Teaching a child at home
- Mentoring a friend
- Quick translation
- Helping a neighbor
- Informal advice or counseling

### Real Examples from Nepal/India

| Contribution | Hours | Proof | Points |
|---|---|---|---|
| Aama teaching granddaughter Nepali language | 2 | Self | ~12 |
| Counseling a troubled youth | 1.5 | Self | ~8 |
| Translating a document for a neighbor | 1 | Self | ~6 |
| Mentoring a student informally | 2 | Self | ~12 |

### How It Works
- ✓ No upload needed
- ✓ Just your word (0.5× proof multiplier)
- ✓ Maximum 100 points per week
- ✓ Usually approved within 1–2 days

### When to Avoid Micro
- ❌ If your work is organized/formal (→ use Standard)
- ❌ If you have strong proof (→ use Standard, get more points)
- ❌ If it took 4+ hours (→ should be Standard)

---

## Standard Track: Light Verification

### What Is It?
Organized, verified contributions. You provide **light proof** — photos, documents, or someone vouches for you.

### Best For
- Classes or workshops (4+ sessions)
- Organized events
- Documented projects
- Verified mentoring programs
- Community service with checkpoints

### Real Examples

| Contribution | Hours | Proof | Points |
|---|---|---|---|
| Running 4-week English conversation class | 4 | Third-party voucher | ~78 |
| Organizing Teej cultural festival | 8 | Photos | ~137 |
| Translating multiple documents (with confirmation) | 6 | Document + email | ~107 |
| Leading volunteer cleanup event | 5 | Photos + participant list | ~90 |

### Types of Proof for Standard Track

| Proof | Example | Multiplier |
|---|---|---|
| **Photo** | Pictures during/after your work | **0.7×** |
| **Document** | Written feedback, confirmation email | **0.8×** |
| **Third-party** | Someone writes: "I confirm X did this" | **1.0×** |

### How to Provide Proof

**Photos** (0.7×):
- Take pictures during or after
- Upload from your phone
- Doesn't have to be professional — smartphone photos are fine!
- 2–3 photos usually sufficient

**Documents** (0.8×):
- Email confirmation from organizer
- Written feedback from participant
- Screenshot of completion
- Any written record

**Third-party Voucher** (1.0×):
- Email from teacher, manager, or participant
- "I can confirm X organized the event and it was great"
- Short statement (1–2 sentences)
- Highest points for Standard track!

### When to Choose Standard
- ✓ You have any of the above proof
- ✓ Work took 4–40 hours
- ✓ It's organized or formal
- ✓ You want more points than micro (upload proof!)

### When to Avoid Standard
- ❌ Only 1–2 hours of informal work (→ use Micro)
- ❌ No proof and can't get any (→ use Micro instead)
- ❌ Work is 40+ hours and institutional (→ use Major)

---

## Major Track: Institutional Proof

### What Is It?
Significant contributions to your community. Requires **strong institutional proof** — official letters, contracts, partnerships.

### Best For
- Building infrastructure
- Major organizational projects
- Partnerships with institutions
- Transformational work
- Work that changed many lives

### Real Examples

| Contribution | Hours | Proof | Points |
|---|---|---|---|
| Building community library in Lalitpur | 40 | Partnership letter + photos | ~1,152 |
| Running scholarship fund (multi-year) | 60 | Official certificate + records | ~1,440 |
| Leading community health initiative | 50 | Hospital partnership letter | ~1,200 |

### Types of Proof for Major Track

| Proof | Example | Multiplier |
|---|---|---|
| **Institutional** | Official letter from organization | **1.2×** |
| **Contract** | Formal agreement showing your role | **1.2×** |
| **Partnership doc** | Letter from school, NGO, hospital | **1.2×** |
| **Certificate** | Official completion certificate | **1.2×** |

### How to Provide Major Track Proof

Get a **formal letter** from an organization:

**Good letter example:**
```
To Whom It May Concern,

This is to confirm that [Your Name] led our community 
library project in Lalitpur. She coordinated with 8 
volunteers, arranged funding, and completed the library 
over 40 weeks of intensive work. The library now serves 
200+ children daily.

Signed,
[Org Director Name]
[Organization Name & Seal]
Date
```

**Just upload this letter.** The system awards the full points with institutional proof (1.2× multiplier).

### When to Choose Major
- ✓ Work took 40+ hours
- ✓ You have (or can get) institutional proof
- ✓ Work had significant community impact
- ✓ You want to recognize transformational effort

### When to Avoid Major
- ❌ Less than 40 hours (→ use Standard)
- ❌ No official proof possible (→ use Standard)
- ❌ Just sharing photos (→ use Standard)

## Choosing Your Track: Decision Tree

```
START
  |
  ├─ Is it informal, under 3 hours?
  │  └─ YES → ⚡ MICRO (honor system)
  │  └─ NO  → continue
  |
  ├─ Is it 4–40 hours with light proof (photo/doc/third-party)?
  │  └─ YES → 📌 STANDARD (light verification)
  │  └─ NO  → continue
  |
  ├─ Is it 40+ hours with institutional proof?
  │  └─ YES → 🏆 MAJOR (institutional)
  │  └─ NO  → Go back to STANDARD (use best proof you have)
```

---

## How Admins Review Each Track

### Micro Track Review (Fast)
- ✓ Read your description
- ✓ Does it sound reasonable?
- ✓ Approve (usually 1–2 days)
- No proof check needed

### Standard Track Review (Medium)
- ✓ Read your description
- ✓ Check your photo/document/email
- ✓ Make sure proof matches description
- ✓ Approve or request clarification
- Usually 3–5 days

### Major Track Review (Thorough)
- ✓ Read your description carefully
- ✓ Check official letter
- ✓ Verify with organization if needed
- ✓ Approve or reject
- Usually 1–2 weeks (due diligence)

## Proof Quality Matters

**Same work, different proof:**

| Proof | Hours | Multiplier | Points |
|---|---|---|---|
| Self-report | 6 | 0.5× | 30 |
| Photo | 6 | 0.7× | 42 |
| Document | 6 | 0.8× | 48 |
| Third-party | 6 | 1.0× | 60 |
| Institutional | 6 | 1.2× | 72 |

**Same activity, 6 hours. With institutional proof, you earn 2.4× more points!**

This is why it's worth getting proper documentation.

## Tips for Each Track

### Micro Tips
- ✓ Keep descriptions honest
- ✓ Realistic hours (don't exaggerate)
- ✓ Include any details that help context
- ✓ Good for frequent, informal help

### Standard Tips
- ✓ Take photos during the activity
- ✓ Get a quick email confirmation ("Thanks for organizing!")
- ✓ Participant feedback counts
- ✓ Most common track for volunteers

### Major Tips
- ✓ Get written agreement from org before starting
- ✓ Document outcomes (photos, metrics, testimonials)
- ✓ Get official letter after completion
- ✓ Include measurable impact ("served 500 people")

## Common Scenarios

### "I taught a workshop. Which track?"
- **1–2 hours, no proof?** → Micro
- **4–6 hours, have photos?** → Standard (0.7×)
- **4–6 hours, students write confirmation?** → Standard (1.0×)
- **10+ hours, series of workshops, institutional proof?** → Major

### "I'm translating documents. Which track?"
- **One quick translation (1 hour), no proof?** → Micro
- **Multiple documents (6 hours), with email confirmation?** → Standard (0.8–1.0×)
- **Ongoing translation service (40+ hours), official partnership?** → Major

### "I built something. Which track?"
- **DIY repair (4–8 hours), photos?** → Standard (0.7×)
- **Community project (20+ hours), photos + testimonials?** → Standard (1.0×)
- **Major infrastructure (40+ hours), partnership letter?** → Major (1.2×)

## Questions?

- **"Can I change tracks after submitting?"** — Yes! Message your org leader. They can update it.
- **"What if my proof doesn't match my track?"** — The system will suggest the right track. You can agree or explain.
- **"What's the minimum hours for each track?"** — Micro is informal (any length), Standard is 4+, Major is 40+. But flexible — ask your admin.
- **"Can I combine tracks?"** — No, one contribution = one track. But you can log multiple contributions!
