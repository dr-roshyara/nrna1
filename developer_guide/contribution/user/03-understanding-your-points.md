# Understanding Your Points

This guide explains how the system calculates your points. It's simpler than it sounds!

---

## The Simple Formula

```
Your Points = (Hours × 10 + Tier Bonus) × Synergy × Proof Quality × Sustainability + Outcome Bonus
```

Don't worry about the symbols. Let me break it down in plain English.

---

## The Four Building Blocks

### 1. Base Points (Hours You Spent)

Every hour of work is worth **10 points**.

| Hours | Base Points |
|---|---|
| 1 hour | 10 points |
| 4 hours | 40 points |
| 10 hours | 100 points |
| 40 hours | 400 points |

This is the **foundation**. Everything else multiplies or adds to this.

### 2. Tier Bonus (Your Track Level)

Bigger tracks get a **recognition bonus** on top:

| Track | Tier Bonus | When? |
|---|---|---|
| **Micro** | 0 bonus | Always (no extra bonus) |
| **Standard** | +50 bonus | When your base is 31+ points |
| **Major** | +200 bonus | When your base is 201+ points |

So:
- **Micro example**: 2 hours = 20 base points → 20 points total (no bonus)
- **Standard example**: 6 hours = 60 base points → **110 points** (60 + 50 bonus)
- **Major example**: 40 hours = 400 base points → **600 points** (400 + 200 bonus)

### 3. Multipliers (Three Factors)

Your points get **multiplied** by three factors:

#### A. Synergy Bonus (Diverse Skills)
Working with people who have **different skills** multiplies your points:

| Your Skills | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Same skill repeated | **1.0×** (no multiplier) |
| 2 different skills | **1.2×** (20% more!) |
| 3+ different skills | **1.5×** (50% more!) |

**Why?** Diverse teams create better outcomes. Teaching (skill 1) + Marketing (skill 2) is stronger than teaching alone.

#### B. Proof Quality (Verification)
Better proof **increases** your score:

| Proof Type | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Self-report (your word) | **0.5×** (half points) |
| Photo (you show it) | **0.7×** (70% of points) |
| Document (written proof) | **0.8×** (80% of points) |
| Third-party voucher | **1.0×** (full points) |
| Institutional proof | **1.2×** (120% of points!) |

**Why?** Strong proof means we're more confident. Institutional proof is best because it comes from an official source.

#### C. Sustainability (Is It Recurring?)
**Recurring work** (weekly, monthly, ongoing) gets a bonus:

| Type | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| One-time event | **1.0×** |
| Recurring / ongoing | **1.2×** (20% more) |

**Why?** Building systems that last is harder and more valuable than one-off events.

### 4. Outcome Bonus (Measurable Results)

After all the multiplying, **admins can add a flat bonus** if you achieved something special:

- Bonus range: **0–200 points**
- When? Only if the contribution had measurable, significant outcomes
- Examples:
  - Library project that serves 500+ people → +100 bonus
  - Scholarship fund that changed 10 students' lives → +150 bonus
  - Community health initiative → +50 bonus

This bonus is **added last**, not multiplied.

## Putting It Together: A Real Example

### Scenario: Teej Cultural Festival

**Your input:**
- Track: Standard
- Hours: 8
- Skills: Event Planning, Marketing, Translation (3 skills)
- Proof: Photos of the event
- Recurring: No (one-time event)
- Outcome: 150 people attended, great feedback

**Calculation:**

```
Step 1: Base
  8 hours × 10 = 80 points

Step 2: Add tier bonus
  Standard track = +50 bonus
  80 + 50 = 130 points

Step 3: Synergy multiplier
  3 different skills = 1.5× multiplier
  130 × 1.5 = 195 points

Step 4: Proof quality multiplier
  Photos = 0.7× (not as strong as third-party)
  195 × 0.7 = 136.5 points

Step 5: Sustainability multiplier
  One-time event = 1.0× (no bonus)
  136.5 × 1.0 = 136.5 points

Step 6: Outcome bonus
  Great event, no outcome bonus this time = 0
  136.5 + 0 = 136.5

Final: 136–137 points (rounded)
```

**You earned 137 points** for organizing the Teej festival!

## Weekly Cap (Micro Track Only)

**If you do micro-track contributions**, there's a **100-point weekly cap**:

- **Week 1**: You earn 50 points (from 3 micro contributions) → Total: 50 ✓
- **Week 2**: You earn 40 points → Total: 40 ✓
- **Week 3**: You earn 70 points, but cap is 100. Your total would be 170, so you only earn 30 this week → Total: 30 ✓ (capped)

**Why?** Micro is the "honor system" track. The cap prevents people from gaming the system with lots of small claims.

**Standard and Major tracks have NO weekly cap** — you can earn as much as you want!

## What Happens After Approval?

Once your org leader **approves** your contribution:

1. ✓ The formula is calculated automatically
2. ✓ Your points are **added to your total**
3. ✓ An entry is written to the **Points Ledger** (permanent record)
4. ✓ Your profile shows your updated score
5. ✓ The leaderboard updates (if you're visible)

## Common Questions

### "Can I earn negative points?"
No. Your points can only be **zero or positive**. The system doesn't take away points (except for rare administrative corrections, which are noted in the ledger).

### "If I don't upload proof, do I get zero points?"
No! You still earn points. But with self-report (no proof), you earn **half the points** (0.5× multiplier). So:
- **With proof**: 6 hours = 60 base, 0.8× proof = 48 points
- **No proof**: 6 hours = 60 base, 0.5× proof = 30 points

Always better to upload proof if you have it!

### "What if I upload the wrong proof?"
Just message your org leader. They can update it or ask you to resubmit. The formula will be recalculated when approved.

### "Do I get points for every hour of work?"
Yes! Even partial hours. 2.5 hours = 25 base points. But the system works best when you're honest about time spent.

### "What if my contribution involved money I spent?"
The system is **points-based**, not money-based. If you spent your own money on a community project, you might mention it in the description (admins can add an outcome bonus for major impacts), but the system doesn't reimburse.

---

## Points Are Permanent

Once approved, your points are **permanent and auditable**:

- ✓ Written to the immutable ledger
- ✓ Can't be deleted (only adjusted by admins)
- ✓ If there's a correction, the original stays and a note is added
- ✓ Your org leader can see your full history anytime

This is why contributions matter so much — they're a **permanent record of your impact**.

---

## Why Does This System Work?

The formula rewards:
- ✓ **Effort** (hours you spend)
- ✓ **Collaboration** (working with diverse skills)
- ✓ **Quality** (proof you have)
- ✓ **Sustainability** (building lasting systems)
- ✓ **Impact** (measurable outcomes)

It balances **trust** (micro track is honor system) with **accountability** (major track needs strong proof).

Everyone's contributions matter, but **significant, verified, sustainable work matters most**.
