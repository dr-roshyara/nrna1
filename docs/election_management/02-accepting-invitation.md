# Accepting Your Appointment

Before you can access any election management pages, you must accept your appointment via the invitation email sent to you.

---

## Step 1 — Find the Email

Look in your inbox for an email with the subject:

> **"You have been appointed as an Election Officer"**

The email is sent from the platform to the email address associated with your account.

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ From:    noreply@yourplatform.com                    │
│ Subject: You have been appointed as an Election      │
│          Officer                                     │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                      │
│  Hello [Your Name],                                  │
│                                                      │
│  You have been appointed as a chief for              │
│  [Organisation Name].                                │
│                                                      │
│  ┌──────────────────────────┐                        │
│  │   Accept Appointment     │  ← Click this button  │
│  └──────────────────────────┘                        │
│                                                      │
│  This link expires in 7 days.                        │
│  If you did not expect this, you can ignore it.      │
│                                                      │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

> **Not in your inbox?** Check your spam/junk folder. Search for "Election Officer" or the platform name.

---

## Step 2 — Click "Accept Appointment"

Click the green **Accept Appointment** button in the email.

### If you are already logged in

You will be taken directly to a confirmation page:

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
│   ✅ Appointment Accepted!           │
│                                      │
│   You are now an active              │
│   Chief Election Officer for         │
│   [Organisation Name].               │
│                                      │
│   [ Go to Management Dashboard ]     │
└──────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Your status is now **Active**. You have full access to your role's features.

---

### If you are NOT logged in (guest)

The page will show the login form with your email **pre-filled**:

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
│   Election Officer Invitation        │
│                                      │
│   Please log in to accept your       │
│   appointment.                       │
│                                      │
│   Email:                             │
│   ┌────────────────────────────────┐ │
│   │ your.email@example.com         │ │
│   └────────────────────────────────┘ │
│                                      │
│   Password:                          │
│   ┌────────────────────────────────┐ │
│   │ ••••••••••                     │ │
│   └────────────────────────────────┘ │
│                                      │
│   [ Sign In ]                        │
└──────────────────────────────────────┘
```

1. Enter your **password** (email is already filled in)
2. Click **Sign In**
3. You will be automatically redirected to the acceptance confirmation

> **Do not change the pre-filled email address.** The invitation is tied to your specific account. Logging in as someone else will result in a "Wrong User" error.

---

## Step 3 — Confirm You Are Active

After accepting, verify your status is active:

1. Go to your organisation page
2. Look for the "Election Officers" section
3. Your name should appear with a green **Active** badge

---

## Common Problems

### ❌ "This invitation has expired"

**Cause:** The link is more than 7 days old.

**Solution:** Ask your organisation administrator to re-appoint you. A fresh invitation email will be sent.

---

### ❌ "Wrong user — this invitation is not for you"

**Cause:** You are logged in as a different account than the one the invitation was sent to.

**Solution:**
1. Log out of the current account
2. Log back in with the email address shown in the invitation email
3. Click the invitation link again

---

### ❌ "You have already accepted this invitation"

**Cause:** You clicked the link a second time after already accepting.

**Action:** No action needed — you are already active. Go to your Management Dashboard or Viewboard directly.

---

### ❌ "Invalid signature" or "403 Forbidden" on the link

**Cause:** The link was modified or corrupted (e.g. by email forwarding or line wrapping).

**Solution:**
1. Go back to the original email
2. Click the button (do not copy-paste the URL)
3. If still failing, ask for a new invitation
